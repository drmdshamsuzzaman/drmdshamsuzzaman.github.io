Research Summary

I am a microbiologist specializing in bacteriophage therapy, antimicrobial resistance, gut microbiome-based therapeutics, microbial genomics, and bacterial taxonomy. My research focuses on developing phage-based and microbiome-informed strategies against multidrug-resistant bacterial infections.

Research Interests

  • Bacteriophage therapy and antimicrobial resistance
  • Gut microbiome and probiotic therapeutics
  • Bacterial taxonomy and genome-based classification
  • Host–microbe interactions and infectious diseases
  • Translational microbiology and in vivo infection models
