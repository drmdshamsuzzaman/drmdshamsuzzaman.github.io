Research Summary

Microbiologist specializing in bacteriophage therapy, antimicrobial resistance (AMR), gut microbiome-based therapeutics, bacterial taxonomy, and microbial genomics. My research focuses on developing phage-based and microbiome-informed strategies against multidrug-resistant bacterial infections.
